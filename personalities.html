Favourite Personalities

Prophet_Muhammad

Prophet Muhammad is considered the last prophet of Islam and a central figure in Islamic religion. Born in Mecca in 570 AD, he received the first revelations of the Quran at the age of 40, which began his mission to spread the teachings of Islam. He preached the message of monotheism and peace, and his teachings emphasized compassion, social justice, and equality. His influence has been felt not only in religion but also in politics, ethics, and culture. He is revered and respected by millions of Muslims around the world, and his legacy continues to inspire people to this day.

Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a prominent political figure and former cricketer who is currently serving as the Prime Minister of Pakistan. He is known for his vision of creating a corruption-free Pakistan and promoting social justice and equality. Imran Khan has been actively involved in philanthropic work, establishing a cancer hospital and university in Pakistan. He also initiated several social welfare projects, such as Ehsaas program, aimed at providing financial assistance to low-income families. He is a vocal supporter of human rights and environmental conservation, and has taken several measures to address climate change in Pakistan. Imran Khan is widely respected both in Pakistan and internationally for his leadership, integrity, and dedication towards creating a better future for his country.

Quaid E Azam

iQuaid-e-Azam Mohammad Ali Jinnah was the founding father of Pakistan and a prominent Muslim leader who led the movement for independence from British rule. He was born on December 25, 1876, in Karachi, British India. He was a skilled lawyer and politician who devoted his life to the cause of Pakistan. Quaid-e-Azam was a man of integrity, strong principles, and unwavering determination. He tirelessly worked towards the creation of Pakistan, and his leadership and vision played a pivotal role in the country's independence. He also served as the first Governor-General of Pakistan, where he worked towards establishing a strong and stable government. His contributions to the creation of Pakistan will always be remembered and celebrated by the people of Pakistan.
